Information Technology Institute

Program grant details

It is possible to register for the specialized professional training program
scholarship at the Information Technology Institute (ITI) online, through the institute’s website. This year’s scholarship targets the 44th batch of graduates from 2019 through graduates of the current year 2023 from various governorates of the Republic.

Fields of study for the Ministry of Communications scholarship

  • Digital shopping
  • Software Engineering
  • Computer networks
  • Geographic information systems
  • Multimedia
  • E-business
  • Graphic design for media
  • Information Security
  • Distance education techniques
  • Mechatronics
